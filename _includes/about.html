About Us

IMPULSE will be a series of exciting events, each leading to another. The primary goal is to provide upcoming engineers a peek into state-of-the-art research and industry practices, and to direct them towards it. IMPULSE has 3 broad focus areas, namely Digital Signal Processing (DSP), Machine Learning (ML) and Computer Vision (CV).

Participants will also be introduced to the different challenges of IEEE SPS and IEEE CS like ICASSP, ICIP, CVPR and ECCV. These sessions will be handled by past challenge winners.

IMPULSE aims to help students sharpen their skills in these fields, with proper mentoring. Students can choose from one of the three tracks for learning.
